Our Vision

Our Target and Goals

The mission of our Chapter is to build a robust Competitive Programming culture on campus and to help our classmates, friends, peers, and juniors to become better problem solvers fostering learning and professional development. We will be conducting different activities and engagements throughout the year to achieve the mission, such as:

  • Bring a competitive programming culture on the campus under the mentorship of CodeChef
  • Organizing events, meetups, and speaker sessions on campus to raise awareness
  • Hosting various contests and competitions to facilitate learning
  • Conducting programming workshops and doubt-sessions for better learning
  • Provide opportunities and exposure to students on the campus

We will bring in some amazing energy on our campus through events like programming contests hosted on the CodeChef, workshops, guest lectures, or fest collaborations that will promote programming and inspire you all. Few activities that we will be doing:

  • Daily Problem Solving and Discussion on Discord
  • DSA Learning Sessions/ Workshops
  • Upsolving Sessions for Monthly Contests
  • ICPC Preparation
  • Workshops and Events for students
  • Programming Contests
